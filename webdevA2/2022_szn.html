The 2022 F1 Season

Relive all the action from the 2022 F1 season! (accurate as of 11/7/2022)

Highlights of the 2022 F1 Season!

Bahrain

Ferrari are back as they win the 1st race of the season!

Saudi Arabia

Max comes back from a DNF to beat Charles!

Australia

Charles won the race with 30 seconds to spare!

Imola

First F1 sprint session of the year and first session with rain added to the mix!

Miami

Ferrari came close to Red Bull, but they werent fast enough!

Spain

Horrible luck for Charles as his engine went up in flames as he was leading the race!

Monaco

Ferrari messed up the strategy, allowing Red Bull to secure 1st and 3rd in the race!

Azerbaijan

Its a double DNF for Ferrari as Red Bull increases their lead in the Constrtuctors Championship!

Canada

Carlos came so close to his first win... Will he get it soon enough?

British

Carlos finally got his first win, with a fantastic batlle for the lead position in the last 5 laps!

Austria

Ferrari are back on the top step of the podium! Will Charles be able to continue his championship charge?
